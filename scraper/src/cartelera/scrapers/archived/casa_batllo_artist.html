2026 Magical Nights

VISIT + DRINK + CONCERT

Audrey

Jazz, Soul, R&B, Disco

@audreyenne

Audrey is a young solo artist who has the gift of transmitting her feelings and who particularly enjoys her concerts at Magical Nights. Her repertoire includes artists such as Whitney Houston, Celine Dion, Alicia Keys, Adele, Beyoncé, Ariana Grande, Rihanna and Dua Lipa.

At each performance, she transmits a mix of emotions that will make you feel special, really touching your heart and following you home.

What most stands out for her at Casa Batlló are the feelings conveyed through the new immersive visit and the magical and cosy atmosphere of the roof terrace.
